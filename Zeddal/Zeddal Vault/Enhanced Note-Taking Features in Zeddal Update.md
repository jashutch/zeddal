# Enhanced Note-Taking Feature in [[Zeddal]]

## Overview
The latest update introduces a significant improvement to the note-taking process in [[Zeddal]], focusing on enhancing user efficiency and note organization.

## Feature Details

### Automatic Note Placement
- Users can now **create a note directly within an existing folder**.
- The new note will automatically be placed in a folder that matches the categorization of existing notes. This eliminates the need for manual sorting, such as copy-pasting or dragging notes into specific folders.

### Transcription Enhancements
- The transcription service has been upgraded to capture audio in a more structured format:
  - **Speaking Confidence**: Previously, transcriptions focused on the confidence level of the recognized words. The system now emphasizes the clarity and assertiveness of the spoken word.
  - **Recording Timestamps**: Transcriptions now include precise timestamps indicating the exact seconds of the recording. This feature aids in locating specific parts of the audio more efficiently.

### Integration with Vaults
- The feature ensures that notes are **automatically created in the appropriate vault**. This integration helps maintain organizational consistency and improves the retrieval process.

## Conclusion
This update not only streamlines the workflow by automating the organization of notes but also enhances the accuracy and usability of transcriptions, making [[Zeddal]] more effective for users in managing their digital documentation.
