# Overview of Zeddal

**Zeddal** is a voice recording interface designed to work seamlessly with note-taking applications like Obsidian and Notion. Its primary function is to transcribe spoken words into text, providing users with a real-time transcript of their spoken language.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Transcription:** Zeddal captures and transcribes natural language speech into precise text as it is spoken.
  
- **Text Refinement and Summarization:** After transcription, Zeddal offers the option to refine and summarize the statements into a concise form.
  
- **Markdown-Friendly Formatting:** The refined summaries are formatted in markdown, making them ready for integration into applications that support markdown syntax. This feature is especially useful for creating structured notes that are easily navigable.

## Integration with Note-Taking Applications

Zeddal supports integration with tools such as Obsidian and Notion, which allows users to:
- **Cross-Link Notes:** Users can link different notes within their knowledge vault, enhancing the organization and retrieval of information.
- **Cross-Reference Information:** This feature aids in building a well-connected knowledge base, facilitating easier access to related information across notes.

By leveraging Zeddal, users can enhance their productivity and improve the management of their digital notes through efficient transcription and sophisticated note structuring.
