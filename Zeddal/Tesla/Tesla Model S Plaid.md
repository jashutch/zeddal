# Tesla Model S Plaid Overview

## Basic Information
- **Model Year**: 2021
- **Base Price**: $129,990 (USD)

## Performance Specifications
- **Powertrain**: Tri-motor all-wheel drive
  - **Configuration**: One front motor and two rear motors
- **Peak Horsepower**: 1,020 HP
- **Peak Torque**: 1,050 lb-ft (1,420 Nm)
- **Acceleration**: 0 to 60 mph in under two seconds
- **Top Speed**: 200 mph

## Battery and Range
- **Battery Capacity**:
  - **Gross**: 100 kWh
  - **Usable**: Approximately 95 kWh (varies by market and conditions)
- **EPA Estimated Range**: Approximately 348 miles (Plaid variant)

## Physical Dimensions
- **Curb Weight**: Approximately 4,560 lb
- **Cargo Capacity**: 28.1 cubic feet
## Introduction
The **Tesla Model S Plaid** was launched in 2021 as Tesla's most advanced electric vehicle (EV) in terms of performance. It's designed to showcase the upper limits of EV technology and speed.

## Performance and Engineering
The Model S Plaid is equipped with a **tri-motor, all-wheel drive system** that generates over 1,000 horsepower. This powerful setup enables the car to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in less than 2 seconds, making it one of the quickest production cars available. It can reach a top speed of nearly 200 mph. Despite this high performance, it maintains a considerable range of approximately 390 miles.

## Technological Innovations
- **Carbon-Wrapped Rotors:** These contribute to the vehicle's ability to handle immense power outputs efficiently.
- **Refined Thermal Management:** Ensures optimal performance by maintaining ideal operating temperatures for the battery and motors.
- **Advanced Aerodynamics:** Helps in reducing drag and enhancing speed capabilities.

## Design and Features
- **Yoke Steering Wheel:** The introduction of this steering wheel has been polarizing among users due to its unconventional design.
- **Upgraded Infotainment System:** Offers enhanced user interface and connectivity features.
- **New Interior Architecture:** Highlights Tesla's commitment to combining luxury with cutting-edge technology.
## Tesla Model S Plaid Battery System Overview

### Battery Specifications
The **Tesla Model S Plaid**, available from 2021 to the present, features a high-performance lithium-ion battery pack. This pack is specifically designed to deliver both extreme output for performance and long-range efficiency for extended driving.

### Battery Pack Details
- **Official Designation**: Battery Pack - High Voltage, 100 Kilowatt Hour, Plaid, All Wheel Drive
- **Part Number**: 1104424-00-E
- **Revisions**: Subsequent minor revisions include 00-F/G in later builds.

### Battery Chemistry and Configuration
- **Cell Chemistry**: Nickel Cobalt Aluminum (NCA)
- **Cell Format**: Cylindrical cells, 18650 format (18mm by 65mm)

### Key Features
- The battery employs a **proven architecture**, which has been retained from earlier Model S variants. This is due to its stability and the maturity of its supply chain, ensuring reliability and continuity in production.

# Tesla Model S Plaid: Year-to-Year Summary (2021-2025)

## **2021: Launch of Model S Plaid**
- **Introduction of Plaid Variant**: Features tri-motor all-wheel drive and delivers 1020 horsepower.
- **Performance**: Accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in under 2 seconds.
- **Palladium Refresh**: Included an updated body design, a new interior screen layout, and a very low drag coefficient.

## **2022: Updates and Refinements**
- **Styling Tweaks**: Minor adjustments to the overall design.
- **Lighting**: Upgraded to matrix LED headlights and redesigned tail lights.
- **Charge Port**: Redesigned cover.
- **Branding**: Shift from the word 'Plaid' to a badge representation.
- **Infotainment Enhancements**: Hardware upgraded with more RAM for gaming, addition of Steam support.
- **Range**: Slight increase in EPA range listings.

## **2023: Stability and Minor Changes**
- **Overall Design and Mechanics**: Largely unchanged from 2022, focusing on carryover of mechanicals and styling.
- **Interior**: Some refinements to screen trim.
- **Performance**: Specifications remained consistent with previous years.

## **2024: Subtle Updates**
- **Range Calculations**: Changes in EPA calculations affected the official range figures, showing slight variations.

## **2025: Significant Aesthetic and Functional Upgrades**
- **Aerodynamics and Styling**: Received revised aerodynamics and new styling elements.
- **New Features**: Included a new front bumper camera and tweaks to the diffuser.
- **Price Adjustment**: Noted an increase in price.

These notes provide a concise overview of the evolution of the Tesla Model S Plaid from its debut in 2021 through to anticipated changes in 2025. Each model year brought specific updates that enhanced both performance and user experience.
