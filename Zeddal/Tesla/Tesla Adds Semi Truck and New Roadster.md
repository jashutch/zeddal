# Tesla's Expanded Product Line

[[Tesla Inc]]., a leader in electric vehicles, has further diversified its product range with two notable additions:

- **Tesla Semi:** An innovative semi-truck designed for freight hauling.
- **Next-Generation Tesla Roadster:** A new version of Tesla's high-performance sports car.

Tesla continues to lead in the EV market by maintaining the broadest selection of exclusively electric vehicles. The company is committed to ongoing refinement and updates across its entire model range.
