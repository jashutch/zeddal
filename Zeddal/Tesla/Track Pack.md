# [[Tesla Model S Plaid]] Performance Enhancements

The [[Tesla Model S Plaid]] offers several options to enhance its performance. One significant option is the **track pack**, which is essential for achieving the vehicle's maximum capabilities. The components of the track pack include:

- **High-performance carbon brakes kit**: Enhances stopping power and endurance during high-speed operations.
- **Sports tires and high-performance sport tires**: Provide improved grip and handling.
- **Optional 20-inch rims**: These larger rims are designed to accommodate the upgraded tires and improve overall vehicle aesthetics.

Importantly, installing the track pack is necessary to unlock the Tesla Model S Plaid's top speed of **200 miles per hour**.
