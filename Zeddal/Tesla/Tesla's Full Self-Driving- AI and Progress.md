# The Future of Full Self-Driving with Tesla

## Tesla's AI and Robotics Infrastructure
Tesla leverages an advanced AI robotics infrastructure for its Autopilot Full Self-Driving (FSD) system. This system is powered by **large-scale training of neural networks**, utilizing:
- 48 different networks
- Tens of thousands of GPU hours

## Progress and Challenges
The development of Tesla's FSD technology is influenced by several factors:
- **Mapping Improvements**: As geographical data accuracy increases, the system's navigation capabilities improve.
- **Regulatory Approval**: Gaining regulatory approval is crucial for higher levels of automation and varies by region, impacting the deployment timeline.
- **Software Enhancements**: Continuous improvements in software algorithms enhance system performance and safety.

## Deployment of Upgrades
Tesla employs **over-the-air software updates** to deploy new features and upgrades. This method allows for:
- Gradual rollout of new capabilities
- Compatibility checks with existing hardware configurations
- Seamless integration for Tesla vehicle owners

**Note:** The timeline for achieving higher levels of automation remains uncertain due to the complex interplay of technological advancements and regulatory frameworks.
