# Tesla Autopilot and Full Self-Driving (FSD) Overview

## Basic Autopilot Features
Tesla vehicles are equipped with a standard feature known as **Autopilot** (also referred to as Basic Autopilot). This system includes several automated functions designed to assist the driver:
- **Adaptive Cruise Control**: Automatically adjusts the car's speed to maintain a safe distance from vehicles ahead.
- **Lane Centering**: Helps keep the car centered in its lane.

## Full Self-Driving Supervised (FSD)
For those looking for more advanced capabilities, Tesla offers an upgrade or subscription to **Full Self-Driving Supervised (FSD)**. This enhanced version provides additional features including:
- **Automatic Lane Changes**: Changes lanes automatically when it is safe to do so.
- **Highway Navigation**: Manages driving tasks on highways.
- **Stop Sign and Traffic Light Handling**: Recognizes and responds to stop signs and traffic lights.
- **City Street Driving**: Capable of navigating through city streets in some markets.
