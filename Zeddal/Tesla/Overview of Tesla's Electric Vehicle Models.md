# Tesla's Electric Vehicle Lineup

Tesla, renowned for its innovation in electric vehicles, has expanded its lineup beyond the well-known Tesla Roadster. Here's a breakdown of their current offerings:

- **Tesla Model 3**: A key player in Tesla's lineup, known for its affordability and performance.
- **Tesla Model X**: Recognized for its distinctive falcon-wing doors and roomy interior, catering to families and luxury seekers.
- **Tesla Model Y**: A compact SUV that combines the affordability of the Model 3 with increased space and utility.
- **Cybertruck**: Tesla's venture into the pickup truck market, notable for its unique angular design and robust features.
- **Cyberquad**: A limited-release, four-wheel drive ATV designed to complement the adventurous and rugged features of the Cybertruck.

All these models are fully electric and feature battery-operated systems. Some models are also equipped with full self-driving capabilities, highlighting Tesla's push towards autonomous vehicle technology.
